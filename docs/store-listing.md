## Detailed Description

### English

PagePulse gives you instant, actionable website analysis — right from your browser toolbar.

**One click. Five dimensions. Zero data collection.**

🔍 **SEO Analysis**
Check title tags, meta descriptions, heading hierarchy, canonical URLs, and OGP tags instantly.

⚡ **Performance Check**
Detect render-blocking scripts, missing image dimensions, lazy loading gaps, and excessive inline styles.

♿ **Accessibility Audit**
Verify alt text coverage, language attributes, form labels, empty links, and viewport settings.

🏗️ **Structure Review**
Validate Schema.org structured data, robots meta tags, HTTPS status, favicon presence, and HTML size.

🤖 **AI Optimization (LLMO)**
Evaluate your site's readiness for AI-powered search engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overview. Check E-E-A-T signals and EC-specific structured data.

**Key Features:**
• 100% client-side — all analysis runs in your browser, no data sent anywhere
• Zero permissions beyond the active tab
• Beautiful, intuitive UI with animated score ring
• Issue prioritization: critical → warning → pass
• Completely free

**Perfect for:**
Web developers, SEO specialists, digital marketers, site owners, and anyone who wants to understand how their website performs at a glance.
