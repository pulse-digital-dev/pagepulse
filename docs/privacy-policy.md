# Privacy Policy — PagePulse

**Last updated:** March 23, 2026

## Overview

PagePulse is a browser extension that analyzes web pages for SEO, performance, accessibility, structure, and AI optimization. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection

**PagePulse does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

Specifically:

- ❌ No user accounts or login required
- ❌ No browsing history tracked
- ❌ No cookies or tracking pixels
- ❌ No data sent to external servers
- ❌ No analytics or telemetry
- ❌ No personally identifiable information (PII) collected

## How PagePulse Works

PagePulse operates entirely within your browser:

1. When you click the PagePulse icon, it injects a content script into the **currently active tab only**.
2. The script analyzes the page's HTML structure (DOM) locally.
3. Analysis results are displayed in the extension popup.
4. **All processing happens on your device. No data leaves your browser.**

## Permissions

PagePulse requests the following browser permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `activeTab` | Access the currently active tab when you click the extension icon |
| `scripting` | Inject the analysis script into the active tab |

These are the minimum permissions required for PagePulse to function. No broad host permissions or background data access is requested.

## Third-Party Services

PagePulse does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or analytics platforms.

If this privacy policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of PagePulse after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
