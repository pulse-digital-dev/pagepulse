FAQ

Can I use it for free forever?
Yes, all Free plan features (30+ analysis checks and export) are completely free forever. You will never be charged automatically.
Does it work on intranets and local development environments?
Yes. PagePulse analyzes the rendered DOM directly without external APIs. It works perfectly behind Basic Auth, corporate VPNs, and on localhost.
How is it different from Lighthouse or other SEO tools?
The key difference is real-time evaluation of the final rendered state (post-JS execution). It accurately analyzes dynamically generated elements in SPAs (React/Vue) and identifies specific actions in seconds.
